# Brazing Wire Family Guidance

Lasernexus recommends material families, not certified grades.

| Family | Typical use | Risks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| CuSi | Steel or coated-sheet brazing-style applications where wetting and appearance matter | Coating burn-off, wetting instability, corrosion validation |
| AlSi | Aluminum brazing-style applications | Oxide layer, cleaning, heat input sensitivity |
| Ni-based | Corrosion or temperature-driven compatible applications | Final grade depends on customer standard |
| Cu-based | Copper-family filler use cases | High reflectivity and heat conduction |
| stainless-filler | Stainless filler welding use cases | Clarify fusion with filler vs brazing |
| custom | Unknown or outside-catalog material pair | Supplier and customer standard validation required |

Final wire grade must be confirmed against customer material standards, corrosion requirements, and trial welds.

Do not treat wire family selection as certified grade approval.
