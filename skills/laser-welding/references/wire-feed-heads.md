# Push-Pull Wire-Feed Head Guidance

Push-pull laser brazing must include head, feeding geometry, wire material family, and validation.

## Key Inputs

- wireFeedMode: push-pull, push, pull, manual-assist
- wireFeedOrientation: front, rear, side, coaxial, near-coaxial
- wireFeedAngleDeg
- wireNozzleOffsetMm
- wireSpeedMmPerS
- headCoolingRequired
- seamTrackingRequired
- preheatRequired
- collisionEnvelopeNotes

## Risks

- TCP interference with fixture or part geometry
- Wire feeding instability
- Wetting instability
- Nozzle access and cooling limitations
- Galvo scanner layouts may not have enough wire-feed head space

## DOE Axes

Validate power, speed, defocus, gap, wire speed, wire angle, preheat, shielding gas, and clamp force.
